Sec. 12 . (a)Each school corporation shall:(1)include in the school corporation's curriculum instruction concerning the human immunodeficiency virus (HIV); and(2)integrate this effort to the extent possible with instruction on other serious communicable diseases.(b)Literature that is distributed to school children and young adults under this section must include information required by IC 20-34-3-17 .(c)The department, in consultation with the state department of health, shall develop HIV educational materials. ?The department shall make the materials developed under this section available to school corporations.
